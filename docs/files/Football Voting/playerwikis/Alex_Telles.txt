<p><b>Alex Nicolao Telles</b> (<span style="font-size:85%;">Brazilian Portuguese:&#160;</span><span title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)" class="IPA"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Portuguese" title="Help:IPA/Portuguese">[alks tlis]</a></span>; born 15 December 1992), is a Brazilian professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)#Full-back" title="Defender (association football)">left-back</a> for <a href="/wiki/Premier_League" title="Premier League">Premier League</a> club <a href="/wiki/Manchester_United_F.C." title="Manchester United F.C.">Manchester United</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Brazil_national_football_team" title="Brazil national football team">Brazil national team</a>.
</p><p>Telles started his career at Brazilian <a href="/wiki/Campeonato_Brasileiro_S%C3%A9rie_D" title="Campeonato Brasileiro Série D">Série D</a> side <a href="/wiki/Esporte_Clube_Juventude" title="Esporte Clube Juventude">Juventude</a>, before being sold to <a href="/wiki/Campeonato_Brasileiro_S%C3%A9rie_A" title="Campeonato Brasileiro Série A">Série A</a> side <a href="/wiki/Gr%C3%AAmio_Foot-Ball_Porto_Alegrense" title="Grêmio Foot-Ball Porto Alegrense">Grêmio</a> in 2013. That year, he was voted the best left-back in the league.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> In January 2014, Telles signed with Turkish <a href="/wiki/S%C3%BCper_Lig" title="Süper Lig">Süper Lig</a> club <a href="/wiki/Galatasaray_S.K._(football)" title="Galatasaray S.K. (football)">Galatasaray</a>, winning various trophies including a league title, two <a href="/wiki/Turkish_Cup" title="Turkish Cup">Turkish Cups</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Turkish_Super_Cup" title="Turkish Super Cup">Turkish Super Cup</a>, only to be loaned to <a href="/wiki/Serie_A" title="Serie A">Serie A</a> team <a href="/wiki/Inter_Milan" title="Inter Milan">Inter Milan</a> during the 201516 season. In July 2016, Telles was sold to <a href="/wiki/Primeira_Liga" title="Primeira Liga">Primeira Liga</a> side <a href="/wiki/FC_Porto" title="FC Porto">FC Porto</a>, where he established himself as one of the best left-backs in the league. He made almost 200 appearances for Porto and won four trophies, including two league titles, one <a href="/wiki/Ta%C3%A7a_de_Portugal" title="Taça de Portugal">Taça de Portugal</a> and one <a href="/wiki/Superta%C3%A7a_C%C3%A2ndido_de_Oliveira" title="Supertaça Cândido de Oliveira">Supertaça Cândido de Oliveira</a>, as well as being named in the Primeira Liga Team of the Year for three consecutive seasons. Telles' performances sparked the interest of several European clubs, and in October 2020, he signed for Manchester United for an initial 18&#160;million (£15.4&#160;million).
</p><p>Born in Brazil, Telles also holds <a href="/wiki/Italian_nationality_law" title="Italian nationality law">Italian citizenship</a>, but ended up representing <a href="/wiki/Brazil_national_football_team" title="Brazil national football team">Brazil</a> at international level. He made his senior international debut in 2019.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alex_Telles&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="Juventude_and_Gr.C3.AAmio"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Juventude_and_Grêmio">Juventude and Grêmio</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alex_Telles&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Juventude and Grêmio">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Telles was born in <a href="/wiki/Caxias_do_Sul" title="Caxias do Sul">Caxias do Sul</a>, a city in the Brazilian state of <a href="/wiki/Rio_Grande_do_Sul" title="Rio Grande do Sul">Rio Grande do Sul</a>. At the age of eight, he began playing football with the neighbouring kids and was later enrolled to a youth academy of <a href="/wiki/Esporte_Clube_Juventude" title="Esporte Clube Juventude">Esporte Clube Juventude</a>.
</p><p>Telles began his professional career with Juventude, making his debut on 24 January 2011 against <a href="/wiki/Esporte_Clube_S%C3%A3o_Jos%C3%A9" title="Esporte Clube São José">São José-PA</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He scored his first goal on 20 August in a 11 home draw against <a href="/wiki/Esporte_Clube_Cruzeiro" title="Esporte Clube Cruzeiro">Cruzeiro</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In December, Telles was transferred to <a href="/wiki/Gr%C3%AAmio_Foot-Ball_Porto_Alegrense" title="Grêmio Foot-Ball Porto Alegrense">Grêmio</a> after a partnership was established with Juventude.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> He made his Grêmio debut on 3 February 2013 against <a href="/wiki/Sport_Club_Internacional" title="Sport Club Internacional">Internacional</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> On 26 May, Telles made his <a href="/wiki/Campeonato_Brasileiro_S%C3%A9rie_A" title="Campeonato Brasileiro Série A">Série A</a> debut, starting in a 20 home win against <a href="/wiki/Clube_N%C3%A1utico_Capibaribe" title="Clube Náutico Capibaribe">Náutico</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Galatasaray">Galatasaray</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alex_Telles&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Galatasaray">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 22 January 2014, after long negotiations, Telles completed a move to Turkish club <a href="/wiki/Galatasaray_S.K._(football)" title="Galatasaray S.K. (football)">Galatasaray</a> for  <a href="/wiki/Euro" title="Euro"></a>6&#160;million, signing a contract that will keep him with the club until 2018.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> He made his debut two weeks later, starting in a 30 win against <a href="/wiki/Tokatspor" title="Tokatspor">Tokatspor</a> for <a href="/wiki/2013%E2%80%9314_Turkish_Cup" title="201314 Turkish Cup">the campaign</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Turkish_Cup" title="Turkish Cup">Turkish Cup</a>, and made his maiden <a href="/wiki/S%C3%BCper_Lig" title="Süper Lig">Süper Lig</a> debut in a 30 home victory against <a href="/wiki/Eski%C5%9Fehirspor" title="Eskiehirspor">Eskiehirspor</a> on 8 February 2014.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Telles made his first assist for the club against <a href="/wiki/Antalyaspor" title="Antalyaspor">Antalyaspor</a> in a 22 away game on 17 February.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> He also scored his first goal for Galatasaray against <a href="/wiki/Akhisar_Belediyespor" class="mw-redirect" title="Akhisar Belediyespor">Akhisar Belediyespor</a> on 8 March in a 61 home win.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In August 2015, there were rumours<sup class="noprint Inline-Template" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Naming_conventions_(geographic_names)" title="Wikipedia:Naming conventions (geographic names)"><span title="The geographic scope near this tag is ambiguous. (September 2015)">where?</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> mentioning Telles as a good choice to fill the place made by the departure of left-back <a href="/wiki/Filipe_Lu%C3%ADs" title="Filipe Luís">Filipe Luís</a> at <a href="/wiki/Chelsea_F.C." title="Chelsea F.C.">Chelsea</a>. In response to the rumour, Galatasaray manager <a href="/wiki/Hamza_Hamzao%C4%9Flu" title="Hamza Hamzaolu">Hamza Hamzaolu</a> said the club would only be tempted by an acceptable offer.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (September 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Loan_to_Inter_Milan">Loan to Inter Milan</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alex_Telles&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Loan to Inter Milan">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>On 31 August 2015, Galatasaray agreed to let Telles join Italian club <a href="/wiki/Inter_Milan" title="Inter Milan">Internazionale</a> of <a href="/wiki/Serie_A" title="Serie A">Serie A</a> on a one-year loan for a 1.3&#160;million fee, in addition to 250,000 if Inter qualify for the group stage of the <a href="/wiki/2016%E2%80%9317_UEFA_Champions_League" title="201617 UEFA Champions League">201617 UEFA Champions League</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-KAP_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-KAP-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> The deal included a buyout option for 8.5&#160;million,<sup id="cite_ref-KAP_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-KAP-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> letting Internazionale make the move permanent when the loan deal is finished.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> Telles reunited with Internazionale manager <a href="/wiki/Roberto_Mancini" title="Roberto Mancini">Roberto Mancini</a> in Italy, who previously coached him at Galatasaray.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He made his debut for the club on 13 September in the <a href="/wiki/2015%E2%80%9316_Serie_A" title="201516 Serie A">201516 Serie A</a> matchday 3 against city rivals <a href="/wiki/A.C._Milan" title="A.C. Milan">Milan</a>, playing the entirety of the match as Inter won 10.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Porto">Porto</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alex_Telles&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Porto">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 12 July 2016, Portuguese side <a href="/wiki/FC_Porto" title="FC Porto">FC Porto</a> announced the signing of Telles for a <a href="/wiki/Euro" title="Euro"></a>6.5&#160;million fee. According to a Galatasaray press release, the <a href="/wiki/Istanbul" title="Istanbul">Istanbul</a> club reserved a further 10% of any added value in a future transfer deal.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> The following day, after signing a five-year contract, he stated: <i>"I ambition titles, I ambition a very beautiful story. I have recently commented with my relatives that I do not want to come here in passing, I want to leave a legacy."</i><sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> On 12 August, in a 31 away win against <a href="/wiki/Rio_Ave_F.C." title="Rio Ave F.C.">Rio Ave</a>, he marked his league debut and first competitive match with a poor performance and subsequent expulsion.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Despite the rough start, Telles gradually cemented himself in the team's starting eleven, scoring his first goal when facing rivals <a href="/wiki/Boavista_F.C." title="Boavista F.C.">Boavista</a> in a 31 home victory at <a href="/wiki/Est%C3%A1dio_do_Drag%C3%A3o" title="Estádio do Dragão">Estádio do Dragão</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Manchester_United">Manchester United</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alex_Telles&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Manchester United">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 5 October 2020, <a href="/wiki/Manchester_United_F.C." title="Manchester United F.C.">Manchester United</a> had agreed a deal in principle to sign Telles for a fee believed to be £15.4&#160;million.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> The transfer was completed and Telles was announced as a United player that day.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> He made his debut playing as a left wing-back on 20 October in a 21 win over <a href="/wiki/Paris_Saint-Germain_F.C." title="Paris Saint-Germain F.C.">Paris Saint-Germain</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2020%E2%80%9321_UEFA_Champions_League_group_stage" title="202021 UEFA Champions League group stage">UEFA Champions League group stage</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-24" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alex_Telles&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Although he was born in Brazil, Telles also has an Italian passport,<sup id="cite_ref-Passport_25-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Passport-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> making him in the past eligible to represent <a href="/wiki/Italy_national_football_team" title="Italy national football team">Italy national team</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Passport_25-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Passport-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> In October 2016, Telles said that he would "welcome" a call-up from the <i>"Azzurri"</i>, adding that his great-grandparents are Italian and he feels Italian.<sup id="cite_ref-26" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> In March 2019, however, he received his first call-up to the <a href="/wiki/Brazil_national_football_team" title="Brazil national football team">Brazil national team</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-27" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> He made his debut for Brazil on 23 March 2019 in a friendly against <a href="/wiki/Panama_national_football_team" title="Panama national football team">Panama</a>, as a starter.<sup id="cite_ref-28" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Style_of_play">Style of play</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alex_Telles&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Style of play">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Telles is an offensive <a href="/wiki/Full-back_(association_football)" class="mw-redirect" title="Full-back (association football)">full-back</a> and is described as a skilled, quick and <a href="/wiki/Playmaker" title="Playmaker">playmaking</a> defender.<sup id="cite_ref-29" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
